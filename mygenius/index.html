Utah Jazz Fanpage

This is for the 3 Utah Jazz fans in the world

The Utah Jazz are a basketball team located in Utah and they play in the NBA. The Utah Jazz were originally known as the New Orleans Jazz which was the original team from 1974-1979. Once the team moved to Utah in 1979 they were a mediocre team up until 1984 when they drafted John stockton and Karl Malone in 1985. They would have 45+ every season up until 1998.

They have never won a NBA championship even though they lost in the finals twice.
