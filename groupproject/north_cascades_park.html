North Cascades National Park has nice lakes and trees!

It's far away, so camping (overnight at least) is recommended.


Ross Lake

North Cascades National Park is home to some stunning scenery, like the Cascade Pass. Nearby are the Ross Lake National Recreational Area and Lake Chelan National Recreational Area, which you will pass while traveling through the North Cascades. It costs $30 for one-time admission, but an $80 National Park pass will get you into any National Park in America for and entire year! Very good deal, if you plan on visiting Rainier and Olympic.
