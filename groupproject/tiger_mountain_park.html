Tiger Mountain is less than an hour's drive from Kirkland; nice day trip!

Tiger Mountain is actually a State Forest, not a National Park! The perk is that there's no fee, unlike Nation Parks.


Poo Poo Point

Tiger Mountain is a great Saturday trip for the whole family! The little ones can watch paragliders jump off the mountain and land gracefully on the ground like birds. Older kids can hike all the way up to the top, on nicely maintained trails. Fun fact: Grey's Anatomy fans may recognize Poo Poo Point as where Derek and Merideth's house is located.


Tiger Mountain Trails

Paragliding is fun and easy at Tiger Mountain! If you have the equipment, that is. You can either climb up to the top, known as Poo Poo Point, which takes about an hour, or you can ride a bus. It's probably better to take a bus since paragliding equipment can be heavy.

At Poo Poo Point, paragliders assemble equipment, put on their harness, and run into the sky! It's fun to watch even if you don't partake.
